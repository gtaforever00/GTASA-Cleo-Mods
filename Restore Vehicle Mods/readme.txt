# Restore Vehicle Mods

Author: gtaforever00

Original Link: https://github.com/gtaforever00/GTASA-Cleo-Mods

Platform: Android

## Description

This mod aims to fix your game by restoring mods when resuming the game.

The mod saves your current car into an INI file, watches for changes to mods,
and loads those mods upon resuming the game.  You must be in the car when the
game quits or it won't restore the mods.

## Installing

Place csa into your cleo folder.
